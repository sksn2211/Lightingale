LMS - lighting Management System

A Lighting Management System (LMS) is an innovative technology that provides advanced control and monitoring capabilities for lighting systems in buildings or facilities.

LIGHTING & HVAC

  • Advanced Enlighted sensors can detect five diffent data points.
  • These digital PIR sensors capture real-time occupancy footprints.
  • LMS includes occupancy savings, daylight harvesting, scheduling, meeting room control, and floor plan-based lighting control.
  • Reduces LMS wiring by up to 90%.
  • Sub-System like BMS can use real-time occupancy to drive HVAC savings.
  • LMS can be on-premise, virtual, or cloud-based.
  • Reduces energy consumption and costs. improves the comfort & productivity of occupants.

SPACE UTILIZATION

  • Efficiently use your space in a building or facility.
  • Light management system optimizes space utilization using occupancy sensors and smart analytics.
  • Lighting is monitored and controlled based on occupancy and activity levels in a space.
  • This reduces energy consumption & costs and improves comfort & productivity of occupants.
  • Analyzing data on space utilization and lighting usage helps building managers identify opportunities for optimization and cost saving.

ASSET TRACKING

  • Uses existing LMS system for asset tracking.
  • Real-time tracking and accurate location of the assets within the location.
  • Advanced location tracking with greater accuracy of locating assets with the use of current technologies such as BLE, UWB, Etc.
  • Asset & inventory management with detailed asset wise usage analytics.
  • Effectively saves time and money with exact and accurate location tracking to a particular location within a particular building or floor.
  • Geofencing alerts & other alerts
  • Inventory management

IoT - Internet of Things

ROOM BOOKING

  • Dynamic Booking: A flexible way to book rooms for individuals or groups.
  • Benefits of tracking and analyzing room occupancy and availability.
  • Access and use the app from any mobile device.
  • Integration with teams and outlook for SSO.
  • Integration with occupancy sensors for auto-release.
  • Amenities, Work Orders, temperature & Light control.

SMART WASHROOM

  • Washroom utilization and cleaning analytics.
  • Janitorial utilization and effectiveness analytics.
  • User feedback for availability of consumables, cleaning effectiveness, etc.
  • Notification of consumable levels for refill or replacement
  • The right level of lighting is provided for occupants' activities
  • Automated triggers for cleaning based on various parameters such as count of people, air quality, etc.

Desk BOOKING

  • Helps employees to find and reserve the space they need in a free address environment.
  • Increse utilization of high cost soft seating.
  • Empower employees to find and collaborate with co-workers in spaces that suit their needs.
  • Understand employee preferences with data insights, iterate and improve the workplace experience

SMART PARKING

  • Parking availability score.
  • Remote parking monitoring.
  • RFID integration for automated access.
  • Multiple format of access - RFID tags, cards, QR code, NFC, Etc.

END USER APP

  • App for employees with their real estate and infrastructure
  • App can include meeting room booking, desk booking, visitor management, real estate updates etc.
  • App allows for employees to poll on lighting and HVAC levels for autonomous and democratic lighting and cooling levels
  • Facility teams view statistics and reports based on employee feedback and usage of real estate
  • Can be integrated for SSO for login authenticity
